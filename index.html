    • Add Task: Enter your task and select a date, then click ➕ Add.
    • Edit Task: Click on the task text to edit it, press Enter to save or Escape to cancel.
    • Mark Complete: Click the checkbox next to a task to mark it as done or undo it.
    • Delete Task: Click ❌ on a task to remove it individually.
    • Delete All: Click 🗑 Delete All to remove all tasks at once.
    • Theme Toggle: Use the 🌙 / ☀️ button to switch between Light and Dark modes.
    • Overdue Tasks: Tasks past their due date will be highlighted in red.
